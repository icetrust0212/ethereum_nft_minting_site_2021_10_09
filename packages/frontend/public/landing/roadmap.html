Roadmap

Wrath of Odin: NFT Wars Official Roadmap

Jul 2021

You’ll find below a detailed, chronological breakdown of the features we intend on incorporating into the Wrath Of Odin project. Further to this, we reserve the ability to include/ alter certain facets of the timeline in order to benefit Wrath Of Odin in accordance with the ever-changing landscape of Non-Fungible Tokens and Blockchain development. Our Roadmap ensures that we stay on track whilst simultaneously providing the community with transparency relating to our current location as it relates to the life cycle of Wrath Of Odin.

Stage 1

Q3 2021

Phase 1

The Wrath of Odin Development Team perform full doxxing measures to ensure heightened transparency and investing confidence from members of the community. This, when executed in tandem with the publishing of our official Wrath Of Odin website, means all necessary information is publicly available. To finish Phase 1, we release our first set of NFTs.

Phase 2

This Phase sees the publication of the token contract for $ODIN, as well as the undertaking of the Private and Public Pre Sales, hosted on DxSales. Pertaining to this, launch has proven to be a sensational success; we filled our allocation and reached the hard cap in under 30 seconds.

Phase 3

Public listings on CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap are completed adding further legitimacy to the project. $ODIN token is officially tradable on PancakeSwap with the Development Team having successfully locked liquidity.

Phase 4

The Team have successfully released the official Wrath Of Odin Telegram sticker set, adding a personal flair and customised touch that community members can use to express themselves! Our first Tier System is released: for investors holding over 1000 $ODIN, they gain access to The Valhalla Lounge, an exclusive, token permission social space where larger investors in the Wrath Of Odin project can meet to discuss ideas, engage actively with Developers and technical experts and learn about Norse mythology. Further to this, Phase 4 sees the first airdropping of NFTs; those that participated in either the Private or Public Pre Sales are gifted with the Spawn Of Fenrir or Heimdall NFT!

Phase 5

This stage sees the Team carry out a recognised external audit as it relates to the $ODIN Smart Contract. With this having been a resounding success, the team begin the process of constructing an interoperability bridge from the Binance Smart Chain across to the world of Ethereum. This ensures increased public awareness of the project as well as improved functionality and accessibility. Alongside this, the Team begin the pursuit of expanded partnerships, seeking to establish liquidity pools with other capable projects.

Stage 2

Q4 2021

Phase 1

We execute a series of planned marketing strategies to increase awareness of Wrath Of Odin, as well as beginning initial planning stages for the construction of our NFT loot box mechanism. We increase community participation, drawing together ideas from the community on how best to develop the Wrath Of Odin project.

Phase 2

At this point, we release a second drop of NFTs, providing the community with fresh content with NFTs having been fully vetted and discussed before production and airdropping begins. We release an official airdrop for the Valhalla Lounge, rewarding our larger investors for their efforts in cultivating a productive and welcoming community environment. These airdrops proved to be immensely popular.

Phase 3

The Midgard Café is officially introduced, our second level tier group for holders of 200 $ODIN. A clear pathway to entry to the Valhalla Lounge is made accessible to community members. Construction of our official Wrath Of Odin discord server and renovated website begins. To top off this section, we carry out more marketing, focusing on Twitter engagement and reaching out to social media influencers.

Stage 3

Q1 2022

Phase 1

Construction of the NFT Loot Box mechanism continues in conjunction with the establishment of our Staking System, build in-house by the Development Team. By providing lock-up incentives, we enable a sustainable future for Wrath Of Odin, as well as rewarding those that support us on our journey of development. More details surrounding staking rewards will be released in due course via our plethora of social media channels.

Phase 2

With all these facets in developmental motion, the Development Team introduce our first franchise Spin-Off, Fat Odin, a series of AI generated NFTs produced on the Solana Blockchain. As per always, the aesthetic quality of the collection is assessed with our community in mind. The purpose of this collection is to expand the scope of the Wrath Of Odin project, attract new investors, engage with an exciting new trend of large collection drops and to provide our community with exciting releases. A bespoke developer constructs the software necessary to provide a fair, AI-driven minting process. Expanded marketing is carried out at the same time as we near the release date of the Fat Odin collection.

Phase 3

The Development Team set about the release of the custom Staking System as well as the potential release of a new, larger NFT collection. Whilst this is happening, development of the Wrath Of Odin podcast begins; members of the community decide its name and are able to enjoy weekly episodes featuring the development team and special guests. This will act as marketing in itself and will create a more holistic, entertaining and educational facet to the Wrath Of Odin project.
